# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in MicroPC a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, precise, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Respect differing experiences and viewpoints.
- Prioritize the health and safety of the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language;
- sexualized language or unwanted attention;
- trolling, sustained disruption, or deliberate intimidation;
- publishing another person's private information without permission;
- conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's private vulnerability reporting channel. Maintainers will review reports confidentially and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, issues, or other participation that violates this policy. Temporary or permanent bans may be applied for serious or repeated violations.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
